ABOUT ME

The Journey

BUILT DIFFERENT
FROM DAY ONE

I'm Thapelo Thoke, a game developer and creative based in Johannesburg, South Africa. I'm currently completing my Game Development degree at SAE Institute Rosebank, where I've been building interactive experiences across Unity, AR/VR, and the web. But my love for UI/UX is where I really shine. I love building interfaces that are intuitive, responsive, and visually appealing.

Before game dev, I was already deep in creative work — 2D illustration, graphic design, video editing, and voice work. Those skills didn't go anywhere. They live inside every project I build, from hand-painted textures in a Joseon-era Unity environment to custom card art in a K-pop survival card game.

My games are personal. Cosmo Clash was built because I love K-pop. Ethereal Golf and We Playing Basketball exist because sports is in my blood. My fighting game prototype exists because I've played too many hours of fighting games like BlazBlue, Street Fighter, Tekken and Mortal Kombat to not try building one.

Outside of code and Unity, I'm the Creative Lead at TA Holdings — a Johannesburg creative agency and events collective. I also co-run It's Chai, a boba tea brand, and DJ K-pop and anime events around Johannesburg. Long-term? I'm building toward starting my own Game studio and collaborating with the TA crew to create enjoyable experiences. The plan is set.

"I build games about the worlds I love."

— Thapelo Thoke
